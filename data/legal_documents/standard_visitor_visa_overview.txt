Standard Visitor visa — Overview (UK)

Summary (general information)
- Standard Visitors can come to the UK for tourism, business, short study, or other permitted activities, usually for up to 6 months per visit.
- Depending on nationality, you may need a visa or an Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA), or you may be able to visit without either.
- You must meet eligibility rules and only do permitted activities; you cannot work or live in the UK through frequent or successive visits.
