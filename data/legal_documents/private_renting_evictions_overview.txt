Private renting evictions in England — Overview (general information)

Summary (general information)
- Landlords in England usually need to follow legal notice and court procedures to evict tenants lawfully.
- Different routes can apply depending on tenancy type and grounds for possession.
- Tenants may be able to challenge invalid notices or unlawful eviction steps.
- Councils and housing advice services can provide support, especially where homelessness risk exists.
