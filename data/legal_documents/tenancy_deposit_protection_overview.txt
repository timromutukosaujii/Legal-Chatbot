Tenancy deposit protection in England and Wales — Overview (general information)

Summary (general information)
- In most assured shorthold tenancies, landlords must protect deposits in an approved tenancy deposit protection scheme.
- Tenants should receive prescribed information about where the deposit is protected and how it can be returned.
- At the end of a tenancy, deductions should follow scheme rules and can be disputed through the scheme process.
- If deposit rules are not followed, tenants may have legal remedies through the courts.
