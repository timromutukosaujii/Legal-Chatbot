Employment tribunals in the UK — Overview (general information)

Summary (general information)
- Employment tribunals decide disputes between workers and employers, such as dismissal, discrimination, and wage-related claims.
- Before many tribunal claims, early conciliation through ACAS is normally expected.
- Tribunal processes include submitting a claim, receiving a response, case management, and a hearing.
- Strict time limits usually apply, so users should check official tribunal and ACAS guidance quickly.
