Graduate visa — Overview (UK)

Summary (general information)
- A Graduate visa lets you stay in the UK after completing an eligible course.
- You must be in the UK, hold a Student (or Tier 4) visa, and your education provider must have told the Home Office you completed the course.
- Duration: 2 years if you apply on or before 31 December 2026, 18 months if you apply on or after 1 January 2027, and 3 years for PhD/doctoral qualifications.
- You cannot extend the Graduate visa, but you may be able to switch to another visa.

Source: https://www.gov.uk/graduate-visa
