Asylum in the UK — Overview (general information)

Summary (general information)
- You should claim asylum if you want to stay in the UK as a refugee because you fear persecution in your home country.
- You normally claim asylum at the UK border on arrival, or as soon as possible if you are already in the UK.
- You will attend a screening where your identity is checked and your claim is registered.

Source: https://www.gov.uk/claim-asylum
