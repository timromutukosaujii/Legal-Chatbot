Unfair dismissal in the UK — Overview (general information)

Summary (general information)
- Unfair dismissal is when an employee is dismissed in a way that is not legally fair under UK employment law.
- Employers should have a fair reason and follow a fair process before dismissal.
- In many cases, employees need a qualifying period of employment to bring an ordinary unfair dismissal claim, though exceptions can apply.
- Time limits apply to tribunal claims, so claimants should check deadlines promptly and use official guidance.
