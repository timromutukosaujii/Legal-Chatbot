Employment status — Worker rights (UK)

Summary (general information)
- Workers are entitled to: National Minimum Wage, protection against unlawful wage deductions, statutory minimum paid holiday, minimum rest breaks, the 48‑hour average weekly working limit (or opt‑out), protection from unlawful discrimination, and whistleblower protection.
- Workers are usually not entitled to some employee‑only rights such as unfair dismissal protection or statutory redundancy pay.

Source: https://www.gov.uk/employment-status/worker
