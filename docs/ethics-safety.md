# Ethics and Safety

- System is informational only, not legal advice.
- Refuse personalised case strategy requests.
- Encourage users to verify cited sources.
- Use only public legal and human-rights documents.
- Do not collect or process private legal case data.

## Participant Consent (Evaluation)

- Obtain informed consent before any survey or testing session.
- Participants are 18+ and can withdraw at any time without penalty.
- No personal legal details are collected; questions are general and anonymised.

## Data Handling

- Store survey responses without names or identifiers.
- Keep data on a password-protected device or university storage.
- Delete raw responses after grading or within an agreed retention period.
