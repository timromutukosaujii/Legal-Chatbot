Human Rights Act 1998 — Overview (UK)

Summary (general information)
- The Human Rights Act 1998 brings Convention rights from the European Convention on Human Rights into UK law.
- Official HMRC guidance highlights Article 6 (right to a fair trial) and Article 8 (right to privacy, including private and family life, home and correspondence) as Convention rights protected in UK law through the HRA.
