British citizenship — Overview (UK)

Summary (general information)
- There are different ways to apply for British citizenship based on your circumstances.
- If you apply, you must have permission to stay in the UK until the decision and citizenship ceremony.
- Being born in the UK does not automatically make you a British citizen; eligibility depends on when you were born and your parents’ circumstances.
