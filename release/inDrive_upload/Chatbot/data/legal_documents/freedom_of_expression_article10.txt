Freedom of expression — ECHR Article 10 (UK context)

Summary (general information)
- GOV.UK ECHR memorandum notes that restrictions on disclosure can engage Article 10 (freedom of expression), and that Article 10 gives strong protection to legitimate journalism and political expression.
- The memorandum also notes that expression linked to foreign‑power interference or harmful disclosures may fall outside Article 10 protections, and any interference must pursue legitimate aims such as national security.
