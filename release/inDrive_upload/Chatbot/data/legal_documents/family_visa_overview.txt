Family visa — Overview (UK)

Summary (general information)
- You need a family visa to live with a family member in the UK for more than 6 months.
- You can apply to live with a spouse/partner, fiancé(e), child, parent, or a relative who will provide long-term care.
- You can extend your family visa if you remain eligible, and may become eligible for settlement after meeting the required time in the UK.

Source: https://www.gov.uk/uk-family-visa
