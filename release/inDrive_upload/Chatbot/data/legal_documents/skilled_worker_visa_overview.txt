Skilled Worker visa — Overview (UK)

Summary (general information)
- You can apply for a Skilled Worker visa if you have a job offer from an approved UK employer and a Certificate of Sponsorship.
- The job must be at an eligible skill level, and you must meet salary and English language requirements.
- This route is for working in the UK in an eligible occupation for an approved sponsor.
