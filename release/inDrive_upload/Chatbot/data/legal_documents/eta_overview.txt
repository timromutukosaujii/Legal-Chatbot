Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA) — Overview (UK)

Summary (general information)
- An ETA lets you travel to the UK for tourism, visiting family, or certain other short visits for up to 6 months. It does not guarantee entry.
- Most visitors need an ETA or a visa depending on nationality and purpose of travel.
- ETA fee: £16, increasing to £20 from 8 April 2026 (per GOV.UK).
