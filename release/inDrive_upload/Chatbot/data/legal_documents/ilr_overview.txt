Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR) — Overview (UK)

Summary (general information)
- Indefinite leave to remain (ILR) is how you settle in the UK. It gives the right to live, work and study in the UK without time limits, and can be used to apply for British citizenship.
- There are different ways to apply for ILR depending on your circumstances (for example work, family, long residence, protection routes).
