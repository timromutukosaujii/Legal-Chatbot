Student visa — Overview (UK)

Summary (general information)
- You can apply if you are 16+ and have an offer from a licensed student sponsor, enough funds, English ability, and (if 16–17) parental consent.
- This visa replaced the Tier 4 (General) student visa.
- How long you can stay depends on course length/level; for degree-level courses at age 18+ it is usually up to 5 years, and below degree level usually up to 2 years. You may be able to extend or switch, including to a Graduate visa.

Source: https://www.gov.uk/student-visa
