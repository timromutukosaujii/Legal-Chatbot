Private renting — Tenant rights and responsibilities (England)

Summary (general information)
- Tenants have rights such as: a safe and well‑maintained home, getting their deposit back (and in some cases having it protected), knowing who the landlord is, living in the property undisturbed, and protection from unfair eviction and unfair rent.
- Tenants’ responsibilities include allowing reasonable access for repairs with at least 24 hours’ notice (unless emergency), paying rent, taking care of the property, and paying agreed charges.
